A virtual reality (VR) simulation training enables learners to apply knowledge and practice setting up in a modern school laboratory. In this training, students will learn to make an experiment on how to study the chemical properties of acid by investigating the reaction between acid and base.

PLATFORM

Oculus Quest, Rift and Vive

TOOLS USED

Unity, SketchUp, 3ds Max, Blender, Substance Painter, Adobe Photoshop

DURATION

4 months

TEAM SIZE

1 people

ROLES

Unity Developer, Programmer, 3D Modeler, Lighting, UI Designer

OVERVIEW

Chemistry VR was the outcome of my Final Year Project 1, aimed at overcoming challenges in STEM or TVET's Training, Simulation, and Education. The application offers a comprehensive solution, enabling students to recognize vital elements in a chemistry lab, apply safe work practices, prepare for experiments, and execute them correctly.

Currently, the project is in its prototype stage and remains incomplete.

In this project, I was responsible for everything including :

  • Created the Robot character model, the rooms in the lab, texturing and made other 3D assets
  • Level construction and decoration for the whole environment
  • Designed all the UI
  • Programmed all the mechanics available such as UI dialogue interaction, opening door with access card, made the card spawn after finished the task given, etc
