OVERVIEW

During my internship at Virtareal Sdn Bhd, I collaborated with three colleagues to develop Tuju Tin AR, a mobile game that adapts a traditional Malaysian game with the latest technology. By infusing augmented reality technology, we created a unique playing experience that is both modern and culturally relevant. Our project aimed to generate income and economic activities while preserving Malaysian tradition in a modern way.

I am proud to share that our project was selected for demonstration at the Ekspo Usahawan Selangor (SELBIZ) in 2022, showcasing the potential of our project to attract attention and generate interest. As a team, we are thrilled with the success of our project and look forward to future opportunities to combine technology with cultural elements.

In this project, I programmed most gameplay systems in the game and framework functionality as well as hooking up third party libraries to add email authentication using Firebase, save game support, google play support, etc. (except for creating the floor detection mechanics, art and audio which I directed my colleagues to create).

Although I did not manage to complete the development of the application, I was able to bring everything to an alpha state for the main gameplay. The alpha version was fully playable and had been playtested before my departure from Virtareal Sdn Bhd.

Features:

  • Compatible with almost every ARCore phones
  • Registration system; Sign up and one-time email login
  • The game consists of 10 levels
  • Each level presenting a unique set of challenges such as wind, varying distances between tins, and animal obstacles
  • Has a floor tracking mechanism that enables smooth placement of tins without any jittering
  • Includes a designated throw-in area where players must remain to throw the sandals
  • Collect coins and trophies through the number of tins have been dropped and number of sandals used
