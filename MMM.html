A story-driven game based on the experiences of a young genius boy who has recently lost his mother. In an attempt to recreate the love and care that his mother used to give him, he creates a robot that mimics the actions of his mother and tries to find happiness in the likeness.

PLATFORM

Oculus Quest, Rift and Vive

TOOLS USED

Unity, 3ds Max, Substance Painter, Adobe Photoshop

DURATION

4 months

TEAM SIZE

3 people

ROLES

Unity Developer, Programmer, 3D Modeller, Lighting & Post-Processing, UI Designer

OVERVIEW

My Mechanical Mother is a Final Year Project 02 that utilizes motion capture technology to bring the animations in the game to life. This technology captures the movements and actions of real-life actors, resulting in realistic and fluid animations for the characters in the game.

In this project, I was responsible for :

  • Created the Robot character model, texturing and made other 3D assets
  • Textured the VR hands and made the hand animations
  • Level construction and decoration for the Lab Scene
  • Created particle effects such as smoke and lightning
  • Designed all the UI on the tablet stand
  • Programmed the game mechanics for both Lab Scene and Piano Interaction
  • Made the lightings and Post-Processing for all scenes
  • Designed the Poster, Concept board and Design board
